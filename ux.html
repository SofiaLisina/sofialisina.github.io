UX skills

During my studies, I have completed a project focused on developing an informational website about Cyprus. The website offers comprehensive information for prospective students, entrepreneurs, employees, and travelers. It provides services such as document legalization, visa and residence permit assistance, business mediation for tax registration, vacation tour planning, and help with rentals.

As part of the project, I conducted UX research, which included:

  • developing persona cards;
  • creating a user journey map;
  • designing a functional site map;
  • lo-fi prototyping (paper sketches and digital wireframes);
  • conducting user research and A/B testing;
  • quantitative analysis: Mann-Whitney test, exact Fisher test etc.

For the user research, I created a survey in Google Forms, invited over 20 participants, and analyzed the results. To conduct A/B testing on the website prototype, one of the questions was randomly selected from two different versions for comparison.
